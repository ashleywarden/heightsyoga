COVID Protocols

At Heights Yoga, the safety of our students, staff, instructors and visitors is our priority. We have implemented the following safe work practices as per WorkSafeBC’s requirements to reduce the risk of COVID-19 transmission:

  • Masks are required to be worn throughout the studio and during class.
  • Physical distancing whenever possible.
  • Providing the control measures needed, including PPE, when physical distancing cannot be maintained.
  • Increased cleaning and disinfecting in the studio

Our COVID-19 protocol is as follows:

Before Class:

  • Arrive at the studio no earlier than 15mins prior to class. Stagered entry into the studio. Student will social distance outside 2m apart. Only 3 - 5 people in the lobby. Please wait for a staff member to direct you to enter.
  • Sign in at the front desk by giving them your name.
  • Sanitize with hand sanitizer as you enter.
  • Please maintain social distance in the studio and enter the practice room once.
  • Sign in at the front desk by giving them your name.

During Class:

  • Sign in at the front desk by giving them your name.
  • Our warm classes, the temperature will be set to between 25C to 28C. We are now offering hot classes, the temperature will be set to between 35C to 38C.
  • Teachers will not be offering hands on adjustments.
  • Regular cleaning of high touch areas between class.

After Class:

  • Please wait for the teacher to direct you at the end of the class.
  • Please leave the studio within 15 minutes after the end of class so that staff can disinfect and prepare for the next class.
